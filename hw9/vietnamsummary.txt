The 2019–20 coronavirus pandemic was confirmed to have spread to Vietnam on 23 January 2020.[3][2] As of 23 April 2020,[update] there were 268 confirmed cases, 224 recoveries, and no deaths. More than 185,000 tests has been performed. Hanoi is currently the most affected city with 112 confirmed cases.[1]
